Presentation

Welcome to the documentation of the Paymetrust API. This documentation describes the different operations you can perform with our API. This section presents the basics to get started with the Paymetrust API, including prerequisites and available environments.

Prerequisites

To access and use the Paymetrust API, please follow the steps below:

Registration on the Paymetrust website

Go to the official Paymetrust website at the following address: https://paymetrust.net/. Fill out the registration form with the required information.

Registration validation

After submitting the registration form, your request will be reviewed by the Paymetrust team. Once your registration is validated, an account will be created for you.

Receiving API access

The information to access your account, including your api_key and your api_password, will be sent to the email address you provided during registration. This information is essential to authenticate your requests to the Paymetrust API.

Using the API

With your api_key and your api_password, you will be able to consume the Paymetrust API by following the provided documentation. Make sure to keep your authentication information secure and only share it with authorized parties.

If you have any questions or encounter difficulties during the registration process, do not hesitate to contact the Paymetrust customer support for help.
